# AI Plantation Research & Development
## Objective: Automated Reforestation via Intelligent Agents

This project investigates the intersection of **GPT-5 autonomous agents** and **precision horticulture**. By leveraging Nexora's automation engine, we aim to:
1.  **Monitor Soil Health:** Real-time telemetry of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels.
2.  **Autonomous Irrigation:** Adaptive water delivery based on local weather forecasts and plant transpiration rates.
3.  **Growth Prediction:** Computer vision analysis to forecast yield and carbon sequestration capacity.
